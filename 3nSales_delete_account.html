Request Account and Data Deletion

For users of 3N Sales, you can request to delete your account and all associated data by following these steps:

Steps to Request Deletion:

  1. Send an email to 3nhodingagent@gmail.com with the subject "Delete My Account".
  2. Include your account username or email registered with 3n Sales.
  3. We will respond within 7 business days to confirm your deletion request.

Data Deleted

We will delete:

  • Your account information (name, email, username)
  • Usage data associated with your account

Data Retained

For legal and operational reasons, some logs or anonymized data may be retained for up to 6 months. No personal identifiers will remain.

If you have any questions, contact us at 3nhodingagent@gmail.com.
